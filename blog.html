Google Play Error Fix 2020

Written by Jeff on 05/20

Make sure your date and time are set correctly Open the Settings menu on your device. Tap Additional settings Tap Date & Time If the box next to “Automatic date & time” is un-checked, tap the box to check it. If the box is checked, Tap the box next to "Automatic date & time" to uncheck it and then tap it again to check it. Press the "Home" button to return to the Android's home screen. Your date and time will be automatically updated.

Solution for Application Not Found Error on USB Flash Drives

Written by Riki on 05/23

1. On the Desktop shortcut or from the start menu, right click My Computer icon and click on “Manage” 2. This will open the “Computer Management Console” 3. Then click on the “Disk Management” on the left panel. 4. On the Disk management console, identify and select the USB drive 5. Right click on it and select “Change drive letter and Paths” 6. Next, Click on “Change” tab. 7. On the Change Drive Letter or Path pop-up, click the dropdown to assign the drive a new letter. 8. Windows will display a warning message, just click on Yes to continue. 9. Now close to exit the windows management console.
